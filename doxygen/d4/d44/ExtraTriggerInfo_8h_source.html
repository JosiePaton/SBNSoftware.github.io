25 /**
26  * @brief Additional information on trigger.
27  *
28  * This data structure holds information from the trigger that has no place in
29  * the standard LArSoft data products (`raw::Trigger` and
30  * `raw::ExternalTrigger`).
31  *
32  * Absolute times are counted since The Epoch and are in UTC time.
33  *
34  * Some "historical" information is available:
35  * * count of gates and triggers from the start of the run;
36  * * count of gates and triggers from the previous trigger;
37  * * timestamps of previous gates and triggers.
38  *
39  * Some historical information is available for different gate selections:
40  * * including only triggers and gates the same source as this trigger source;
41  * * including triggers and gates from any source (tagged as "any").
42  *
43  */
