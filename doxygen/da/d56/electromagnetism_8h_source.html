52  * @name Charge quantities
53  *
54  * These charge quantities are tied to `util::quantities::units::Coulomb`.
55  * A few options are provided:
56  *
57  * * most general template, `scaled_coulomb`, allowing to choose both the
58  * scale of the unit (e.g. `std::pico` for picocoulomb) and the type of
59  * the numerical representation
60  * * generic templates (e.g. `coulomb_as`), allowing to choose which numerical
61  * representation to use
62  * * double precision (e.g. `coulomb`), ready for use
63  *
64  */
65  /// @{
