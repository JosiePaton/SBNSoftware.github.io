Detailed Description

Prints the content of optical detector waveforms on screen.

Example of usage:

dump::raw::OpDetWaveformDumper dump;
dump.setIndent(" ");
for (raw::OpDetWaveform const& waveform: waveforms)
dump(mf::LogVerbatim("dumper"), waveform);
